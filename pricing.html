FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in each edition?

Each edition includes different levels of content and features. Please refer to the edition descriptions for specific details.

Can I upgrade my edition later?

Yes, you can upgrade your edition at any time. Please contact our support team for assistance.

Do the editions include future updates?

The Ultimate Edition includes all future updates and new assets at no extra cost. Other editions may require an upgrade or separate purchase for future content.

Is multiplayer supported in all editions?

Yes, all editions support multiplayer features. However, some advanced multiplayer modes and exclusive content may only be available in Premium, Ultimate, or Editors Cut editions.

Can I transfer my progress if I upgrade?

Your game progress and saved data will transfer automatically when you upgrade to a higher edition. No data will be lost.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and select local payment options. All transactions are securely processed.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes, we offer a 14-day refund policy for all editions, provided the game has not been extensively played or content downloaded.

Are there any discounts available?

We occasionally offer discounts and promotions. Please sign up for our newsletter to stay informed.
